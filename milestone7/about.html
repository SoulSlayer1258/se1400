About Route Mapper

When I needed to come up with an idea for a website my first thought was to do something with rock climbing. Rock climbing was a hobby that me and my mom picked up just as something to do and it quickly became something that we look forward to doing every week. Rock climbing has become something that I am able to enjoy doing and it is something that I want to share with others. This is why I wanted to create a website for helping people find routes and have an easier time getting into rock climbing. Route Mappers goal is to make rock climbing easier to get into and to create a place where people can go if they don't know where to start with rock climbing. Route Mapper is a website that can be used by all experience levels, so whether you are just getting into rock climbing or you are an experienced climber you can definitely find something here for you. If you are new to rock climbing you can search for easier climbs and find helpful descriptions to make climbing them easier. You can also search for specific areas, so if you don't want to go on a long drive you can search for rock climbing locations that are nearby. You can also share your own routes and create your own markers and descriptions for them to help other people find climbs just right for them. Of course you can also save specific routes that you may want to come back to and the most popular routes will always be on the front page of the website. At the end of the day Route Mapper just wants to make the experience of rock climbing easier, more accessible, and more fun for everyone to enjoy.

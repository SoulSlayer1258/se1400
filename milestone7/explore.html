Need Some Help Navigating?

This page is simply for finding routes that you may want to go and climb. Simply zoom out and then zoom in to around where you may want to go climbing. Look for places that may have lots of markers in a small area as those places would likely be good climbing locations. You can click on a marker to see what the routes are named, a rough estimate of the route's grade, and a short description of the route itself. Use these features to help find a route that you would like to go climb, and remember to be safe.

After clicking on a marker you can click on the save button to save a route you can sort by your saved routes on the Routes page. This is in case you are looking around and find multiple routes that you want to climb, so you can save them for later.

If you are out climbing and you stumble across a route that is not listed in Route Mapper go to the Create page to give the route its own marker. All you need to fill out are the route's name, an estimate of the grade, and a short description of the route so others can find it. Adding more routes to Route Mapper is beneficial to everyone, including the site, as it's always important to have more places to climb.

If you have any other questions about the site visit the About page for more information about Route Mapper. If you are still having issues with the site visit the Contact page to get some help. Simply fill out a form with your issues, report a bug, or simply give some feedback.

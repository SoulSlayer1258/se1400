Audience

Primary Audience

The main audience for this website will be rock climbers who are interested in exploring the outdoors. Whether they have been climbing for a long time or are just getting into it, this site will be for anyone who wants to go rock climbing outside.

Geolocation

The main audience will likely live in places where rock climbing is readily available with outdoor climbing areas.

Age

The most common age range for this website would likely be about 18-26, which is everyone from college kids to young adults who do rock climbing as a hobby, though the age range could be much lower or higher.

Gender

Rock climbing has been a male dominated sport for a very long time, so that would likely be the majority of my users, that being said women in rock climbing have been increasing in recent years.

Demographics

Some common demographics for the users of my site would be: beginner/intermediate rock climbers, people who are interested in outdoor activity, people who have been climbing in a gym and want to try outdoor climbing, and rock climbers who enjoy creating and sharing routes.
